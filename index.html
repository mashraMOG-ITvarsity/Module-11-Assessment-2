Restaurant Gorden Ramsay: Ravioli of Lobster

The Ravioli Lobster with Salmon, poached in a light bisque featuring lemon longrass and chervil velouté, alongside a side of tomato chutney, is a culinary delight. The combination of perfectly cooked seafood, nuanced flavors, and the refreshing touch of lemon and chervil create a harmonious and sophisticated dish. Each bite is a burst of exquisite taste, earning it a solid 9 out of 10 for its impeccable execution and elevated dining experience. Highly recommended for those seeking a refined and memorable culinary adventure.

Conerstone: Hake Kiev

The Hake Kiev at Cornerstone Restaurant is a culinary delight, featuring perfectly cooked Hake encased in a golden, crispy breadcrumb crust with a flavorful garlic-infused buttery filling. Skillfully executed, this dish earns a solid 8 out of 10 for its delectable taste and creative take on a classic. Highly recommended for those seeking a memorable dining experience with a delightful twist

Benares: Baked Malabar Scottish Scallop

The Baked Malabar Scottish Scallop at Benares Restaurant is a culinary gem, showcasing a perfect balance of flavors and textures. The Malabar spices enhance the natural sweetness of the Scottish scallop, creating a delightful experience. Meticulously prepared and beautifully presented, this dish earns a commendable 9 out of 10 for its exquisite taste. Highly recommended for those in search of a sophisticated and flavorful seafood indulgence.

Chapter One by Mickael Viljanen: Sublime BBQ Donegal Lobster

The Sublime BBQ Donegal Lobster at Chapter One by Mickael Viljanen is an exceptional culinary experience. The perfectly grilled lobster, glazed with cocoa pod concentrate and a touch of jalapeño, offers a unique and sensational flavor combination. Viljanen's innovative approach shines through, delivering a perfect balance of smokiness and sweetness. With flawless execution, this dish earns a remarkable 9 out of 10, making it a must-try for those seeking a distinct and memorable dining adventure.
